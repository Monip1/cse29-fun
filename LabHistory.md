# Lab History

{: .note}
Hi I'm Elena, and the following is written almost entirely from memory. If you find any inaccuracies please let me know.

{: .fun-fact}
The voice of the lab is mainly a combination of the Spring staffs from all 3 years: Eric Xiao, Michael Peng, Elena Tomson and their teams.

1. Before CSE 29 was first thought of, there was a class called CSE 15L. The iteration that I (Elena) took it was Winter 2022 ([CSE15L WI22 website](https://ucsd-cse15l-w22.github.io/index.html)) and that is the quarter that 29 was based on most strongly when 29 labs were written for the first time in [Spring 2024](https://cse29sp24.github.io/).
1. [Spring 2024 Labs](https://cse29sp24.github.io/docs/labs/lab1.html) were written by our original Lab TA with the help of his tutor team who are as follows:
    * Lab TA: [Eric Xiao](https://cse29sp24.github.io/docs/instructors.html#teaching-assistants), 
    * Tutor Team: [Almog Bar-Yossef, Elena Tomson, Angela Tsai, and Harvey Yang.](https://cse29sp24.github.io/docs/instructors.html#tutors)
1. [Fall 2024 Labs](https://ucsd-cse29.github.io/fa24/week1/index.html) is the quarter I did not do lab dev as only TAs did lab dev. They did not use much of what had been created in Spring but you may find a few pieces (like pokemail) from Spring. As my recollection and a brief look at who made the commits to the labs in the [github](https://github.com/ucsd-cse29/fa24) the main lab authors are as follows:
    * TAs Janet Vorobyeva and Yash Garde
1. [Winter 2025 Labs](https://cse29winter2025.github.io/lab1)   upon the return of Gerald came the return of the Gerald labs, ie Spring '24 labs. We updated the labs from SP24 a bit, making room for the practice skill demos and review quizzes that were now to take place during lab. Bits of Fall can likely be found sprinkled about. These are written / updated by:
    * Lab TA [Michael Peng](https://sites.google.com/ucsd.edu/cse29winter2025/staff?authuser=0)
    * Tutor Duo: Senior head tutor [Steven Shi and Elena Tomson](https://sites.google.com/ucsd.edu/cse29winter2025/staff?authuser=0#h.wmhwhwqzybay) as head tutor (even though I'm older than he is although he retired from CSE 29 first)
1. [Spring 2025 Labs](https://cse29spring2025.github.io/lab1) changed very little from winter, a few reworded things here and there as well as moving the ssh key setup to ieng6 way further up than week 7. (no, we don't have a good reason why we had it all the way in week7) Our team this quarter was:
    * Lab TA: [Cora Coleman](https://cse29spring2025.github.io/docs/instructors.html#teaching-assistants)
    * Head tutors [Steven Shi and Elena Tomson](https://cse29spring2025.github.io/docs/instructors.html#tutors) (yes the senior title was just completely dropped that quarter)
1. [Summer 2025 Labs](https://ucsd-cse29.github.io/ss1-25/) were a little different. By a little I mean we don't talk about this quarter. Turns out doing everything remotely in 5 weeks isn't a great idea, who knew? -- in sum it was 4 hour zoom lab using mainly a google doc. There were 17 special in-person students where it was much better but still not the same as the regular 10 x 2 hour labs. I do not recall precisely how much we pulled from each prior quarter for these but I'm positive that these were not refered to in the future labs.
    * Lab TA: Joey Wu
    * Tutor: Elena Tomson

1. [Fall 2025 Labs](https://ucsd-cse29.github.io/fa25/week1/lab1.html) saw a shift to severely reduced staff and the introduction of whiteboards. Lab was shifted to a demo-task-demo-task format where a TA would demo something and the students would do a very similar task using what they just learned. This time we had 4 TAs, each week 2 of which were in charge of writing lab.
    * TAs: Elena Tomson, Andrew Pan, Janet Vorobyeva, and Rachel Lim
1. [Winter 2026 Labs](https://ucsd-cse29.github.io/wi26/week1/lab1.html) saw mild updates from falls labs, polishing them and their demos. The lab team for this quarter:
    * TAs: Elena Tomson and Shaurya Raswan
    * Tutor: Brendan Barber
1. [Spring 2026 Labs](https://cse29spring2026.github.io/lab1) built off of Spring '25 almost entirely, with the introduction of whiteboards, with a few warmup activities pulled from Winter '26. Lab 9 based off of a 15L lab from it's last iteration, all others based on SP25.
    * Lab TA: [Elena Tomson](https://cse29spring2026.github.io/docs/instructors.html#teaching-assistants) 
    * Tutors: [Sam(uel) Gonzalez and Travis Henry](https://cse29spring2026.github.io/docs/instructors.html#tutors)
1. Fall 2026
    * Team remains to be seen :)
